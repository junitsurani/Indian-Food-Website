Health and Safety


AceFoods is serious about the quality of food we serve on a daily basis.

We also carry a Level 4 Award in Food Safety in Catering certificate which you can contact us to view at any point of time. Some of the procedures we undertake to maintain Health and Safety are:

-Cover our hair while cooking

-Wear gloves while packing

-Food temperature check

-Quality check

-Washing hands as and when needed
