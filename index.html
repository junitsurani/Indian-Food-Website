About Us

We Leave A Delicious Food For You

A Warm Welcome from AceFoods. Here at AceFoods we have a true passion to cater for Home Cooked Indian Food. With us you can expect high standard of quality home cooked food at an affordable price.Whether it be a long day after studies or work, or just not being in a mood to cook. When you want to have a meal delivered, as well as want it to have a value for money. Missing “MAA KE HAATH KA KHAANA” or just tired of random takeaways, AceFoods , the Indian Food Service is your “Go to” place.

Not only the amount of time that you save, which you would have spent cooking or looking for takeaway joints, but also the affordability and the convenience of having food delivered to your doorsteps, all of these qualities make us the absolute favourite for our customers.It all started as an initiative from the family. As chef explains, “My brother had recently moved to London and faced problem of having good home-made Indian food.” An idea then conceptualised of starting a Tiffin Service in Cardiff to help students and professionals with homemade food and providing them the much-needed comfort that they get at home, while being away from home. That is why we say, AceFoods is your home away from home!


The Chef, a home science graduate from Chandigarh, Punjab (North India), has a passion for cooking. As her daughter says, and we quote: "She just had to taste something once, and had to have us say that it’s amazing, and she would replicate the exact same dish at home! We don’t feel the need to step out!" The chef regularly updates her food hygiene and health and safety certifications here in the UK, holds a proud 4 on a 5 rating.


AceFoods specialises in providing Home Cooked Lunch/Dinner service to students and professionals based in Cardiff (Wales). We have now been operating for more than 4 years in Cardiff. Our Mission is to provide the best homemade food at an affordable price. Our speciality is that we do not use any tinned food everything is cooked and prepared fresh on daily basis. The biggest quality guarantee that can be assured as our family eats the same food that we deliver to you.


We deliver food from Monday to Friday every week. Our menu changes weekly to satisfy your taste buds with all different varieties of authentic Indian dishes. We are widely popular amongst International students for our delicious, mouth-watering and hygienic home cooked food, and are proud to say that we have served 4 different year groups by now. All of the customers that we have served until now have learnt about us through word of mouth, and that, according to us is our biggest achievement. With minimal marketing, we have successfully survived in the market for more than 4 years, which we consider a success of the small idea that was born due to a family member moving away.
