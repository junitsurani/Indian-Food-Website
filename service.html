Our Service


At Acefoods,we deliver home cooked Indian food to your doorsteps in Cardiff. Prices start from £4.00 and are different for each area. Please contact us before placing an order for more details.Currently we are only operational in Cardiff,Wales. You can select if you want lunch or dinner or both (please note lunch deliveries will be restricted to certain areas only, please call to check if we cover your areas) You can select the meal plan (minimum 5 meals to maximum 20 meals plan) – we do offer one day trials however the price you pay will not be discounted.


Check our weekly menu on our websit Facebook page or request it by sending quick text or WhatsApp message.


We only cook for the day what is required as we do not believe in wasting food. We request you to place your daily, weekly or monthly orders at least day in advance. You can pay advance cash for your dinner and lunch plan on day 1 with your delivery or through bank transfer. All areas within Cardiff are covered during lunch/dinner time subject to delivery charges.


We specially design our packages customised to cater the needs of professionals and students. We price them in fair way to satisfy the craving for home-made vegetarian food. However, we are flexible to altering the food packages customised according to your requirements. Please feel free to contact us to discuss your individual or group requirements.


Please call us to check if we cover your area.
